Let’s create a Slow Street on Steiner between Duboce and Waller

Share your input here

Why Slow Steiner?

  • There are four affordable senior housing buildings within two blocks of Duboce Park. Many residents have expressed fear over fast-moving cars and unsafe street crossings. Our neighbor Michael was recently hit in his wheelchair by a car speeding down Steiner and turning up Duboce Ave. A safer and slower Steiner would provide residents of all ages and abilites a safer connection to the park.
  • Slow Steiner is a pedestrian and bike artery across the city. A Slow Steiner would better support these already common uses of this stretch of Steiner, reducing conflcits between cars, bikes, and pedestrians.
